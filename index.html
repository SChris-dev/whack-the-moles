How to play?

Your lawn is destroyed by the naughty moles!

CLICK!

You and your trustworthy hammer should teach them a lesson, just click on the moles that pop out from the ground! (don't worry the hammer is soft.)

But becareful though! if you hit anything other than the moles, your hammer will break a little, and eventually you can't use it anymore if it breaks.

HIT AS MANY AS YOU CAN!

The moles will retreat after 30 seconds, make sure to hit as many moles so that they will learn their lesson!

Good luck!
